Records-Management-as-a-Service (rMaaS) is a model where an organisation needs to organise and structure content and manage the lifecycle of this content within an organisation fo the purposes of records management

Where do we fit in?


rMaaS is used to provide you with auditing, migration of content, clean-up, labelling and reorganisation of corporate records so that your business can meet its EU regulatory requirements.
